Bankner, Delhi

Bankner

Bankner is situated in the northern part of Delhi. The pincode of this locality is 110040. This locality is near Narela Mandi, Shivaji Nagar and Swatantra Nagar. The residents of Bankner rated this locality at 3.8/5 in terms of connectivity, whereas for safety, they rated it 3.6/5.

Demographics

Historical population:

Year Pop. ±%
1981 6,735 —

As of 2001 India census,[2] Bankner had a population of 21,085. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Bankner has an average literacy rate of 80%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 85% of the males and 75% of females literate. 16% of the population is under six years of age.
